About us




Patel's Vintage Furnishing is a furniture restoration
service committed to providing quality craftsmanship
and preserving the beauty of furniture for generations.
We use only the finest materials and craftsmanship
to make sure each piece is given the utmost care
and attention. Our mission is to provide customers
with unique, hand-crafted furniture that will last for
years. We strive to make the process of finding
and restoring furniture as easy and enjoyable as
possible.
Come experience the difference at Patel's Vintage
Furnishing and enjoy your furniture for years to come.
