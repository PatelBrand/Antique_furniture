Refund policy


We want you to be completely satisfied with your purchase from our antique furniture website.

If for any reason you are not satisfied, we accept returns within 30 days of the delivery date.

If you wish to return an item for a refund, please contact us with your order number and reason for

the return. We will provide you with a Return Authorization (RA) number and instructions on how to
return the item.

All returned items must be in their original condition and packaging, with all accessories and tags included.
Please pack the item securely to avoid damage during shipping. The customer is responsible for all return shipping costs.


Once we receive the returned item, we will inspect it to ensure it is in its original condition. If the item is in its original
condition, we will issue a refund for the purchase price, minus any shipping costs. Please note that the refund will be
issued to the original payment method used to purchase the item.

If the item is damaged or defective upon receipt, please contact us immediately. We will arrange for a replacement
or refund as appropriate.

Please note that custom orders or personalized items cannot be returned or refunded.

By placing an order with us, you agree to abide by our refund policy. If you have any questions, please don't hesitate to contact us.
