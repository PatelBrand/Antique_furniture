Shipping policy


Shipping Methods:
We offer several shipping methods for your convenience, including standard shipping, expedited shipping, and white glove delivery.
Please note that shipping times and costs may vary depending on the shipping method you choose.

Standard Shipping:
Our standard shipping method is curbside delivery, which means that the furniture will be delivered to the curb or driveway outside
of your home. You will be responsible for bringing the furniture inside and setting it up.

Expedited Shipping:
We also offer expedited shipping for an additional fee. Please contact us for more information on expedited shipping options and costs.

White Glove Delivery:
For large or delicate items, we offer white glove delivery service. This service includes delivery of the furniture to the room of your
choice, assembly, and removal of all packaging materials.

Shipping Costs:
Shipping costs are calculated based on the weight and size of the item, as well as the shipping method you choose. Please note that
additional charges may apply for white glove delivery or expedited shipping. Most items ship within 3-5 business days of receiving
your order. Please note that shipping times may vary depending on the item and the shipping method you choose. Once your order has
shipped, we will provide you with a tracking number so you can track your shipment.

International Shipping:
We currently do not offer international shipping. By placing an order with us, you agree to abide by our shipping policy. If you have any
questions, please don't hesitate to contact us.
